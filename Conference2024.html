2024 Virtual Conference Completed

The 2024 virtual meeting of the International Association of Cryospheric Sciences Working Group, focusing on the dynamic processes in the Marginal Ice Zone, has successfully concluded.

The conference brought together leading experts and researchers to explore and discuss the most recent developments in our understanding of the Marginal Ice Zone and its critical interactions within polar regions. This meeting marked the first in a series planned through 2028, setting the stage for ongoing collaborative research efforts.
